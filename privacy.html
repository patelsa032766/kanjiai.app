Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Introduction

KanjiAI is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the KanjiAI mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

KanjiAI is an offline-first application that requires no account and collects minimal data. Here's what we do collect:

Information You Provide:

  • Study Progress: Your flashcard performance, lesson range, and learning statistics are stored locally on your device only.
  • Settings: Your app preferences (such as language settings or audio preferences) are stored locally on your device.

Information Collected Automatically:

  • No tracking: KanjiAI does not track your activity, collect device identifiers, or send your learning data to external servers.
  • No analytics: We do not use analytics tools that track your app usage.
  • No advertisements: KanjiAI does not display ads and does not use ad networks.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since KanjiAI stores all data locally on your device and does not collect data externally, we have no information to use or share. Your learning progress and preferences remain entirely on your device.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local storage only: All app data (flashcards, progress, settings) is stored exclusively on your device using iOS's secure local storage.
  • No cloud sync: KanjiAI does not sync your data to cloud servers or back up your progress online.
  • Device security: Your data is protected by your device's native security measures (iOS encryption, biometric authentication, etc.).
  • No servers: KanjiAI does not operate or maintain backend servers that store user data.

4. Third-Party Services

KanjiAI is offline and does not integrate with third-party services that collect personal data. The app uses:

  • Apple's iOS APIs: For text-to-speech (AVSpeechSynthesizer) and local storage. Apple's privacy policy governs these services.
  • No external libraries for tracking or analytics.

5. Children's Privacy

KanjiAI is appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we store all data locally and do not require any account or personal information, there is no risk of collecting information about minors.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Data access and deletion: Since all your data is stored on your device, you have full control. You can:

  • Access your study progress anytime through the app.
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app or resetting the app's data in iOS Settings.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of KanjiAI after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Summary

TL;DR: KanjiAI is offline-first and privacy-first. We store all your learning data locally on your device. We do not collect, track, share, or sell any information about you. Your privacy is fully protected because your data never leaves your device.
